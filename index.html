About DENNIS

Seeking truth beyond conventional wisdom

Who Am I?

I'm a lifelong seeker of truth, unafraid to question established narratives and explore uncomfortable ideas. My journey has taken me through academia, various professions, and extensive travels, all of which have shaped my perspective.

My Philosophy

I believe in the power of independent thought and the importance of intellectual honesty. Too often, we accept ideas because they're popular or convenient rather than because they're true. My writing aims to challenge these automatic assumptions.

Education & Background

  • BA in Political Studies from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology
  • Former teaching assistant at the Department of History and Political Studies
  • Author of two books on epistemology
  • Contributor to various independent journals

Why I Write

In an age of information overload and ideological polarization, I believe we need more voices willing to think outside conventional frameworks. My goal isn't to tell you what to think, but to encourage you to think more deeply.
